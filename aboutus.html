Oromia Development Assosation Special Boarding School

ODA is one of non governmental organization found in Ethiopia which focus on community development and charity! ODA is a local and membership-based established in 1993 to improve the lives of peoplein Oromia region through sustaible integrated community-based programs. ODA primarily focuses on livelihod diversification,community empowerment,public health promotion,neducation, provision of potable water, environmental protection and promoting voluntarism to contribute to the development of the region. nowadays ODA is working on education sector by building schools in diffrent parts of Oromia and also by opening boarding schools in Adama and Bishoftu. Our moto is unity for development!

Mission

Our Mission is providing high quality education, nature talent and innovation to develop moral and spritual well being of each student in preparation for life of service.

Vision

Our Vission is to see ODA Boarding school becoming the fountain of best minds to produce dedicated future leaders, professional who serve humanity in a diverse national and global society.
